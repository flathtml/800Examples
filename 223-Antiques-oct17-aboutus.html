Tim has lived in Connecticut all of his life. He and his wife, Amy, raised three children in Wethersfield.

Antiques have been a big part of Tim's life as he has always been passionate about History. He has traced his lineage back to 1635 in America and earlier to England.

As a volunteer for the local historical society, Tim coordinated a run of Antiques Shows to raise funds for operating expenses and an acquisition fund.

Away from his regular job, Tim has been an Antiques Dealer for the last twenty-five years, having exhibited at shows throughout New England.
