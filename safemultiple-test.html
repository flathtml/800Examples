Safe Multiple Value Swing Trading system
What is Safe Multiple?

Safe Multiple is a Value Swing Trading system. Its core strategy is to make bets that have least downside risk. The system is built on the principle of “Factor of Safety”. This Principle is laid out in the textbook Security Analysis By Graham and Dodd.
What is Safe Multiple's Goal? To develop a Stock Trading System that consistently beats the major indexes (S&P-500, NASDAQ-100 and Dow Jones Industrial average) over 5 year periods.

Overview of Value Swing Trading process.

STEP-1. Develop a Watch list of stocks with good fundamentals.One should also understand the underlying business of the stock and how it makes money.
STEP-2. Value each stock of the watch list, the valuation would determine the price at which the stock would be undervalued, in essence will have ample factor of safety and least downside risk. This is the price at which the stock would be bought.
STEP-3. Figure out the exit points, that is the price at which the stocks are fully valued. This is the price at which the stock is sold.
STEP-4. Wait patiently till the stocks become undervalued. Only then buy it, and don’t be greedy if the stock appreciates in short time, sell it at the price that was determined before. On certain conditions the stock is sold prematurely. (a) If a defect is discovered in the valuation (b) The fundamentals are drastically changed and the valuation is no more valid.
