Ulta Beauty Valuation :$13.5 billions, $233/ share
Shares outstanding : 58 million  Valuation Date : 9/9/2019
CORE ASSUMPTIONS

1. Currently the company has around 1,200 stores.In 10 years Ulta Beauty would have 2,000 stores each having sales of around $10 million thus having a net Sales of $20 billion.

2. In 10 years the USA beauty Products industry will grow from the current figure of $86 billion to $140 billion. This is based on the data that the Industry grew from $52 billion in 2009 to $86 billion in 2018.

3. In 10 years Ulta Beauty would have 15% of the USA beauty products market. This is based on the data that the company's market share has grown from 2.5% in 2009 to 7.5% in 2018.

4. Growth in Sales/store of 5%, growth in number of stores of 6% for next 10 years , Capital Expenditures per new store of $3.19 millions with additional working capital of $0.60 millions.
