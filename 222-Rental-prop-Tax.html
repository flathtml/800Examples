Rental Property Income Tax

Quick Reference Guide as to how one can report Federal Taxes for Rental Property Income
 
IRS Pub 527 IRS Publication on Rental Property Income Taxes.
IRS Form 1040 : Line 8(a) one needs to report additional Income

IRS 1040 Schedule-1 : Line 5 is used to input net Real Estate Income. Grand total of this form is fed to Line 8(a) of Form 1040.

IRS Schedule E : It is used to calculate Net Real Estate Income. The Grand total is fed to Line-5 of Schedule-1.    Click here forIRS Schedule E Instructions
