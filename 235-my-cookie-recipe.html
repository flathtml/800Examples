Crispy Chocolate Chip Cookies recipe

Dayanand Menashi

 
STEP#1 Preheat oven to 325F

STEP#2 Mix the following:
1 1/3 cup flour
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup melted butter
1 cup light brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
2 eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla
1 cup chocolate chips

STEP#3 Scoop 1 inch balls on parchment lined cookie sheets and bake until the edges are brown.

STEP#4 Cool for a few minutes before removing from sheets.
