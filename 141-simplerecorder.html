    USER'S GUIDE

    1 : Click on RECORD button to start recording.

    2 : Click on STOP button when you are done recording.

    3 : The audio player will be displayed wherein you can listen to your recording.

    4 : To share your audio file, you will need to click on upload link.It will create a shareable link that you can share with others.

    5 : To download the audio file to your device you can click on the Save to Disk link.

    6 : You can also create multiple recordings and choose the best one to share.
